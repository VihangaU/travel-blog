Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

Sigiriya, also known as Lion's Rock, is an ancient rock fortress located in the central province of Sri Lanka. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is famous for its stunning frescoes, water gardens, and impressive architecture.

Highlights

  • Climbed the iconic Lion's Rock
  • Admired the ancient frescoes
  • Explored the beautiful water gardens
  • Learned about the history of King Kashyapa

Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka

Anuradhapura is one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, famous for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Lankan civilization. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is known for its stupas, monasteries, and sacred fig tree.

Highlights

  • Visited the sacred Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi tree
  • Explored the ancient stupas like Ruwanwelisaya
  • Discovered the ruins of ancient monasteries
  • Learned about the history of early Sri Lankan civilization
